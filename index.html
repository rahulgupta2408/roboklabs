What We Do

Robok Labs is a product development and manufacturing company delivering turnkey electronic product design solutions throughout the entire product development lifecycle — from concept to mass production.

Our capabilities span hardware design, embedded firmware, software development, application design and development, mechanical design, industrial design, graphic and UI/UX design, prototyping, testing support, qualification and certification, high volume manufacturing, box build assembly, and PCB assembly.

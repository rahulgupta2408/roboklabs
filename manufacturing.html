ELECTRONICS MANUFACTURING

Precision PCB assembly, box build, and contract manufacturing — manufactured here, done right.

Your Manufacturing Partner in Canada

Robok Labs operates a state-of-the-art electronics manufacturing facility in Toronto, Canada. We offer comprehensive contract manufacturing services — from prototype quantities to high-volume production runs — with an unwavering commitment to quality and traceability.

Our IPC-certified team, advanced inspection technology, and rigorous quality management system ensure that every board and assembly we build meets your exact specifications.

Manufacturing Services

PCB Assembly

From design to prototyping to full-scale manufacturing. Our PCB assembly services cover SMT (Surface Mount Technology), through-hole, and mixed-technology assemblies for a wide range of board complexities and volumes.

  • SMT & through-hole assembly
  • Prototype to high-volume production
  • Lead-free & RoHS compliant processes
  • Full BOM management & component sourcing
  • Automated optical inspection (AOI)
  • In-circuit testing (ICT) & functional test

Box Build Assembly

Seamless system integration services for complete product box build assembly. We handle the full assembly of your end product — from PCBAs and wiring harnesses to final enclosure assembly, labeling, and packaging.

  • Full system-level integration
  • Cable & wire harness assembly
  • Enclosure assembly & hardware installation
  • Functional system testing
  • Custom labeling & serialization
  • Retail-ready packaging & kitting

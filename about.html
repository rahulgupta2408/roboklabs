ABOUT ROBOK LABS

Toronto's premier product development and manufacturing house — engineering your success since day one.

Who We Are

Robok Labs is an electronics product design and development company focused on turning ideas into scalable, production-ready products.

We work with startups, SMEs, and industrial companies to design and build end-to-end electronic systems—from concept to manufacturing.

Our strength lies in bridging the gap between engineering and real-world deployment.

What We Do

At Robok Labs, we don't just design circuits—we build complete products.

Our capabilities include:

  • Electronics hardware design (analog + digital + power systems)
  • Embedded firmware development (real-time, low-power, connected systems)
  • PCB design and prototyping
  • Industrial automation and control systems
  • Product architecture and system design
  • Design for Manufacturing (DFM) and Design for Testing (DFT)
  • Vendor sourcing and manufacturing support

From early-stage prototyping to full-scale production, we take ownership of the entire lifecycle.

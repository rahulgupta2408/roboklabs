ENGINEERING DESIGN SERVICES

Engineering design services for electronic and software product development

End-to-End Product Design

Robok Labs is a Toronto-based company specializing in turnkey, end-to-end custom electronic product design and embedded engineering services. Our portfolio includes 300+ completed projects across medical, automotive, industrial, agritech, cleantech, wearables, and consumer electronics.

From initial concept through design, prototyping, testing, and certification, our multidisciplinary team delivers exceptional results on time and on budget.

Our Design Services

🔧

Electronics Hardware Design

Internal electronics essential for the success of your product. Our hardware engineers design schematics, PCB layouts, BOM selection, and DFM-optimized designs ready for production.

💾

Firmware Development

Smooth, reliable firmware programmed directly into your hardware. From bare-metal embedded C to RTOS-based firmware, we ensure your device performs flawlessly.

⚡

FPGA Design

Enhance your designs with high-performance FPGA solutions. Our team handles VHDL/Verilog development, IP core integration, timing analysis, and hardware-software co-design.

💻

Software Development

Silky smooth code for any language, architecture, or system. Backend services, cloud integration, APIs, and desktop applications — built with maintainability and scalability in mind.

📱

App Development

Complement your product with a responsive and elegant web and mobile application. iOS, Android, and cross-platform apps that seamlessly communicate with your hardware.

🎨

Industrial Design

Drawings and 3D models that validate the look and feel of your idea. Our industrial designers create compelling, market-ready product aesthetics and enclosure designs.

🔩

Mechanical Design

Ensuring all parts function reliably and efficiently. CAD modeling, tolerance analysis, material selection, and DFM reviews ensure your mechanical components are production-ready.

🖥️

UI/UX Design

Creating delightful user experiences for your product's interfaces. Wireframing, prototyping, usability testing, and pixel-perfect design systems for web and mobile.

🔬

Prototyping

The most cost-efficient method for conceiving a physical prototype. Rapid prototyping, 3D printing, PCBA prototypes, and functional demonstrators to validate your concept early.

🧪

Testing Support

Testing is an integral part of our development process. Unit testing, integration testing, environmental stress testing, and EMC pre-compliance testing to ensure product reliability.

✅

Qualification and Certification Services

Designing your product to meet qualification standards. We support FCC, CE, UL, CSA, IC, and other regulatory certifications — navigating the full certification process from pre-compliance to approval.

Our Design Process

A transparent three-phase process with full customer control at every stage.

1

Concept & Research

We begin by deconstructing your design problems, understanding your market requirements, and assembling the right engineering team with relevant expertise for your specific product domain.

2

Design & Development

Expert engineering and technical architecture planning. We execute design iterations with rigorous review cycles, ensuring the solution meets your functional, cost, and manufacturability targets.

3

Collaboration & Support

Transparent communication and ongoing partnership support. We keep you involved throughout, provide regular updates, and offer long-term engineering support post-launch.
